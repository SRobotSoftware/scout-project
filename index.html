At Camp Morrison in McCall, Idaho

Come join our NYLT course! We need leaders just like YOU! Adult Leaders, let us do the talking for you! If you like the course and you’re interested in having scouts from your youth attend, our staff would be happy to come to one of your meetings and present the NYLT program to your unit. We’d be pleased to come and answer any questions you or your scouts might have. We also have some short videos available about the course; we can even play some team games with your troop. The choice is yours.

The course this year will cost $200 per participant. This fee covers meals and the costs for the program elements. A $50 non-refundable deposit is required with an application. Final payments need to be received in the Scout Office by June 20, 2016. We understand that the fees can be steep for some scouts with limited resources; we encourage youth to start now to acquire these funds via work or scholarship (a limited amount are available - all applications will be reviewed by the training committee and awarded as funds allow). We really want all youth leaders to have the opportunity to participate in this fabulous course.
